WELCOME TO LEVEL 43 — VISUAL-BLOCK BULLETING
============================================

🏆  Mission  
Add the bullet “- ” in front of **every** line below using **one** Visual-Block
operation (no macros, no repeats).

────────────────────────────────────────
📝  Step-by-Step

1.  Go to the first character of the block (top line).

2.  Enter Visual-Block mode:
        <Ctrl-v>

3.  Move down to the last line (inclusive).

4.  Insert the bullet in front of the block:
        I- <Esc>

    (That’s capital **I**, hyphen, space, then `<Esc>`.)

    Vim prepends “- ” to every selected line.

5.  Save & quit:
        :wq
────────────────────────────────────────
✅  Success checklist  
    • Every line **begins** with “- ” (hyphen, single space).
