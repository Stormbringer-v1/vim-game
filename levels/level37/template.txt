WELCOME TO LEVEL 37 — SORT WITH “!sort”
=======================================

🏆  Mission  
Alphabetise the list below **from inside Vim** by sending the block
through the external command `sort`.

────────────────────────────────────────
📝  Step-by-step

1.  Visually select the five fruit lines
        V  ↓ ↓ ↓ ↓        (or use `vip` if cursor already on the block)

2.  Pipe the selection through `sort`
        !sort<Enter>

3.  The lines reappear, sorted A→Z.

4.  Save & quit
        :wq
────────────────────────────────────────
✅  Success checklist  
    • The block is in strict alphabetical order:
        apple
        banana
        kiwi
        orange
        pear
