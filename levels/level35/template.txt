WELCOME TO LEVEL 35 — YOUR FIRST AUTOCOMMAND
============================================

🏆 Mission  
Create an autocommand that automatically appends the line

    # SAVED

to **log.txt** every time you save that file.

────────────────────────────────────────
📝 Step-by-step

1.  Open (or create) a scratch rc just for this level:
        :e .vimrc_level35

2.  Add these lines exactly:

        augroup L35
          autocmd!
          autocmd BufWritePost log.txt call append(line('$'), '# SAVED')
        augroup END

3.  Write & source:
        :w | :source %

4.  Open the file you’ll test on:
        :e log.txt
        iInitial content<Esc>

5.  Save log.txt:
        :w
    → A new line  # SAVED  appears at the bottom.

6.  Quit Vim:
        :qa
────────────────────────────────────────
