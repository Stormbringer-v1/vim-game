WELCOME TO LEVEL 2 — DELETING LINES
===================================

Goal: Delete the noisy line inside the task area, then save and quit.

────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Move with arrow keys ← ↑ → ↓ (or h j k l).
2. Move the cursor onto the line that says:  NOISE_NOISE_NOISE
3. Press:
      dd
   to delete the **entire line**.

4. Save and quit:
      :wq <Enter>
