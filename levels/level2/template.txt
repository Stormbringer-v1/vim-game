WELCOME TO LEVEL 2 — DELETING LINES
===================================

🏆  Mission  
Delete the noisy line from the text block — and save your changes.

────────────────────────────────────────
📝  Step-by-step

1. Press `Esc` to ensure you're in Normal mode.
2. Move the cursor to the line that says `NOISE_NOISE_NOISE`.
3. Press `dd` to delete the entire line.
4. Type `:wq` and press Enter to save and quit.

────────────────────────────────────────
✅  Success checklist  
- [ ] The noisy line is completely gone
- [ ] The good lines are untouched
- [ ] You saved and exited with `:wq`
