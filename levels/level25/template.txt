WELCOME TO LEVEL 25 — WINDOW MANAGEMENT
=======================================

Goal • open a vertical split, move between windows, resize them.

────────────────────────────────────────
1.  Split the window vertically:
        :vsplit

2.  Move to the right window:
        <Ctrl-w> l

3.  Make the right window wider:
        <Ctrl-w> >

4.  Make it narrower again:
        <Ctrl-w> <

5.  Quit all:
        :qa
────────────────────────────────────────
