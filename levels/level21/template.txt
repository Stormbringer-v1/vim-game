WELCOME TO LEVEL 21 — CODE FOLDING
==================================

🏆  Mission  
Use manual folding to organize each function block in this file.

────────────────────────────────────────
📝  Step-by-step

1. Move to the line with `BEGIN FUNC`.
2. Press `zf` and move the cursor down to `END FUNC`, then press `Enter` — this creates a fold.
3. Repeat for the second function block.
4. Use `zo` to open folds and `zc` to close them.
5. Save and quit with `:wq`.

Tip: Manual folds are helpful for visual structure — and fully under your control.

────────────────────────────────────────
✅  Success checklist  
- [ ] Two folds were created successfully
- [ ] You used `zf` and practiced `zo` / `zc`
- [ ] You saved and exited cleanly
