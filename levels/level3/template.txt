WELCOME TO LEVEL 3 — MOVING BETWEEN WORDS
=========================================

Goal: Move to the mistake using word motions (`w` or `b`), fix it, save and quit.

────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Move between words:
    - Press `w` to jump forward by one word
    - Press `b` to jump backward by one word

2. Find the misspelled word:  MISTAEK

3. Press `i` to enter INSERT mode.

4. Fix the typo:  MISTAEK → MISTAKE

5. Press <ESC> to leave INSERT mode.

6. Save and quit by typing:
    :wq <Enter>
