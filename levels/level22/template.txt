WELCOME TO LEVEL 22 — CLEANING UP TODOS
=======================================

🏆  Mission  
Replace every `TODO` with `DONE`, using your preferred method.

────────────────────────────────────────
📝  Step-by-step

1. Use `:g/TODO` to find relevant lines.
2. Option A: Manually edit each line (`cw` over `TODO`).
3. Option B: Use substitution: `:%s/TODO/DONE/g`
4. Save and quit with `:wq`.

Tip: Use substitution when you know exactly what you're replacing.

────────────────────────────────────────
✅  Success checklist  
- [ ] All `TODO`s replaced with `DONE`
- [ ] Other content left untouched
- [ ] File saved and exited correctly
