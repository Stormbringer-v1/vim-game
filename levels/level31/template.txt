WELCOME TO LEVEL 31 — ADVANCED MACROS
=====================================

Goal • record ONE macro that converts every  TODO:  line
into an unchecked markdown task  - [ ] …  and then replay it
to process the whole list.

────────────────────────────────────────
1.  Place the cursor on the **first** TODO line.
2.  Start recording into register  a
        qa
3.  Inside the recording:
        dw             " delete the word  TODO:
        i- [ ] <Space> " insert markdown checkbox
        <Esc>
        j              " move down to next line
4.  Stop recording
        q
5.  Replay the macro once
        @a
6.  Replay repeatedly on the remaining lines
        @@  @@  @@     (or   3@@  for three lines)
7.  Save & quit
        :wq
────────────────────────────────────────

<<TASK>>
TODO: buy groceries
TODO: finish report
TODO: call Alice
TODO: schedule meeting
TODO: water plants
<<END>>
