WELCOME TO LEVEL 31 — ADVANCED MACROS
=====================================

🏆  Mission  
Use a macro to update each line from `TODO:` to `DONE:`.

────────────────────────────────────────
📝  Step-by-step

1. Move to the first line and start recording: `qa`
2. Use `^cwDONE` to change the word `TODO`, then press `j`
3. Stop recording with `q`
4. Replay with `@a`, or repeat with `@@` until finished
5. Save and quit with `:wq`

Tip: If you mess up, use `u` to undo and re-record the macro.

────────────────────────────────────────
✅  Success checklist  
- [ ] All lines now begin with `DONE:`
- [ ] Macro was used to repeat the change
- [ ] You saved and exited properly
