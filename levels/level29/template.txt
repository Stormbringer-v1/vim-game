WELCOME TO LEVEL 29 — SPELL CHECK
=================================

🏆  Mission  
Use Vim's built-in spell check to fix all the errors.

────────────────────────────────────────
📝  Step-by-step

1. Enable spell check with `:set spell`
2. Move the cursor to each misspelled word (they’ll be red/underlined).
3. Use `z=` to open suggestions, and pick the correct one.
4. Repeat for all lines, then disable spell check with `:set nospell`
5. Save and quit with `:wq`

Tip: Use `]s` and `[s` to jump to the next/previous misspelling.

────────────────────────────────────────
✅  Success checklist  
- [ ] All misspellings are fixed
- [ ] Spell check was enabled and used
- [ ] You saved and exited properly
