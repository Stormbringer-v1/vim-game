WELCOME TO LEVEL 9 — RECORDING MACROS
=====================================

🏆  Mission  
Use a macro to remove all the `BAD LINE`s efficiently.

────────────────────────────────────────
📝  Step-by-step

1. Move to the first `BAD LINE`.
2. Press `q` followed by a letter (like `a`) to start recording a macro into register `a`.
3. Press `dd` to delete the line, then `j` to move to the next line.
4. Press `q` again to stop recording.
5. Now replay the macro by pressing `@a`. Run it multiple times with `@@` or `3@a`.

Use the macro to remove all `BAD LINE`s — quickly.

────────────────────────────────────────
✅  Success checklist  
- [ ] All `BAD LINE`s are removed
- [ ] You used a macro (not manual deletion for each one)
- [ ] You saved and exited Vim properly
